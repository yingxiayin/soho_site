<p>Choosing the right material for your outdoor furniture is crucial for durability and longevity. Different materials offer various benefits and maintenance requirements.</p>

<h2>Aluminum</h2>
<p>Aluminum is lightweight, rust-resistant, and requires minimal maintenance. It's ideal for modern designs and can withstand various weather conditions.</p>

<h2>Teak Wood</h2>
<p>Teak is naturally weather-resistant and develops a beautiful silver-gray patina over time. It's durable and requires minimal maintenance, making it a premium choice.</p>

<h2>Synthetic Materials</h2>
<p>Modern synthetic materials like resin wicker and plastic offer excellent weather resistance and are often more affordable than natural materials.</p>

<h2>Steel and Iron</h2>
<p>Powder-coated steel and wrought iron provide strength and durability, but require proper maintenance to prevent rust in humid climates.</p>
